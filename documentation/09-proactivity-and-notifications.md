# docs/09-proactivity-and-notifications.md
**Proactivity & Notifications – Sam MVP**

## 1. Scope

Sam may initiate contact only in narrow, user-relevant commute moments.

## 2. Proactivity principle

Offer a session, do not force one. One opportunity per commute.

## 3. Notification model

Soft prompt, system reminder, session status notification.

## 4. When to notify

Only during valid commute windows or visible long-running operations.

## 5. Work scheduling

Use WorkManager sparingly for persistent deferrable work.

## 6. Battery strategy

Avoid continuous location polling and frequent wakeups.

## 7. Notification content

Short, useful, localized.

## 8. Response actions

Start session, dismiss, snooze, open app.

## 9. Foreground vs background

Use in-app prompts when possible; notifications for background re-entry.

## 10. Permission and compliance

Ask clearly, respect opt-out immediately.

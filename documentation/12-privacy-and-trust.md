# docs/12-privacy-and-trust.md
**Privacy & Trust – Sam MVP**

## 1. Scope

Privacy posture of the local-first MVP.

## 2. Privacy principles

Collect only what the MVP needs; keep core data on-device.

## 3. Data categories

On-device product data, temporary processing data, sensitive data.

## 4. What Sam does not collect

No contacts, calendar, email, ad IDs, analytics by default, raw audio archive by default.

## 5. Permission philosophy

Ask at the moment of need, explain benefit, allow reduced mode.

## 6. Local-first trust model

Core session data stays on-device; no backend required.

## 7. Data safety disclosure

Play Data safety form must match actual behavior.

## 8. Transparency UX

Explain privacy in onboarding and settings.

## 9. Audio handling

No silent recording, no background capture, no default uploads.

## 10. Location handling

Use only for commute detection and only when enabled.

## 11. Retention policy

Tasks and sessions remain until deleted; temp transcripts only as needed.

## 12. Security basics

Keep sensitive data out of logs and source control.
